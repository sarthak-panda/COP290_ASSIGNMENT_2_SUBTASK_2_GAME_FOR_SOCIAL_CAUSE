Social Importance

The game carries significant social importance. It promotes empathy, responsibility, and care for the elderly, as seen in the boy's actions towards the old man. It emphasizes the importance of timely medical care and the role each one of us can play in ensuring the well-being of those around us.

The game also highlights the importance of education and discipline, as the boy balances his social service with his schoolwork. The visit to the temple introduces a spiritual element, encouraging players to consider their spiritual health alongside their physical and mental health.

However, the game concludes with a reminder that while exercise is important, it's equally crucial to listen to one's body and understand its limits. This could be interpreted as a message to balance physical activity with adequate rest, promoting a holistic approach to health and well-being. This change further enhances the game's social relevance by promoting a balanced lifestyle.

In conclusion, this game serves as a powerful tool for promoting social values and responsibilities among its players, making it not just a source of entertainment but also a medium for social change.
